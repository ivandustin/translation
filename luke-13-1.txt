There were some who reported to Jesus about the Galileans whose blood Pilate had mixed with their sacrifices.
Luke 13:1

Do you think that these Galileans were worse sinners than all the other Galileans because they suffered this?
Luke 13:2

No, I say to you; but if you do not repent, you will all likewise perish.
Luke 13:3

Or those eighteen on whom the tower in Siloam fell and killed them: do you think that they were worse debtors than all the other people living in Jerusalem?
Luke 13:4

No, I say to you; but if you do not repent, you will all likewise perish.
